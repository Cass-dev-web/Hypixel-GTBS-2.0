Guess the Build Word List Solver

What is this?

Guess the Build Solver is a word list cheat for all the themes on the Hypixel gamemode.
By entering the hints given at the 90 (1:30) second mark,
the solver will return a list of all the possible words for that hint combination.
For more information, click the "Help & Usage" button above.

Simply enter the hints in the textbox, including underscores(_).
Tip: For spaces, do not use an underscore for better results.

  • Usage
  • Simply enter the hints in the textboxes as they appear in the game.
    Words that matches the hints will be automatically shown in the results section.
  • Try all the words in the list and you'll end up guessing what it is.
    Out of the list, you should guess which one looks like the build first.
    You can also click on the word to copy it to the clipboard.
